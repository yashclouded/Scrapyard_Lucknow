About Us

Scrapyard Lucknow is a premier hackathon event designed exclusively for high school students. Our mission is to inspire young innovators and provide them with a platform to showcase their creativity, problem-solving skills, and technical abilities.

This non-profit event brings together talented high schoolers for 12 intense hours of coding, collaboration, and creation. Participants will have the opportunity to work in teams, develop innovative solutions, and compete for amazing prizes.

Scrapyard Lucknow is more than just a competition - it's a learning experience where students can network with peers, interact with industry mentors, and push the boundaries of their technical knowledge.
